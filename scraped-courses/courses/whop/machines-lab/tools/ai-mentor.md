# Machines Lab AI Mentor

**Type:** Interactive AI Tool (Whisp Chatbot)
**URL:** https://whop.com/joined/machines-lab/machines-lab-ai-mentor-kTZkWjhsC6QbAQ/app/
**Name:** Machina

## Overview

The Machines Lab AI Mentor is an AI chatbot trained on the content from all Machines Lab courses. It serves as a personalized tutor and Q&A assistant for course members.

## Purpose

As mentioned in the welcome post on Machines Hub:
> "Machines Labs AI Mentor: an AI trained on the content from the courses"

The AI Mentor helps students:
- Get answers to questions about course content
- Clarify concepts from the 7-day prompt engineering course
- Get help with prompt engineering challenges
- Receive guidance on applying course concepts

## Features

- **Course-Trained AI:** Specifically trained on Machines Lab course materials
- **Chat Interface:** Interactive Q&A format
- **Nanobanana Integration:** Can generate images
- **Chat History:** Saves conversations for reference
- **24/7 Availability:** Always available to answer questions

## How to Use

1. Navigate to the AI Mentor page
2. Type your question in the "What do you want to know?" field
3. The AI will respond based on course content and prompt engineering knowledge
4. Continue the conversation for follow-up questions

## Recommended Use Cases

- "How do I apply the three-question framework?"
- "Explain context layering from Day 2"
- "Help me improve this prompt: [your prompt]"
- "What's the difference between zero-shot and few-shot?"
- "How do I design effective role constraints?"
